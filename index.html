About Us

Where engineering meets asset management

DAMM Capital builds and operates quantitative financial infrastructure on-chain. We research, build and deploy quantitative financial vehicles that execute autonomously through smart contracts. Transparent, permissionless, and fully auditable.

The same engine runs both sides of DAMM — our tokenized funds, and the integrations that take fintechs into DeFi further, faster. Our team combines deep technical expertise with rigorous risk management. We don’t predict markets. We engineer systems that adapt to them.
