Strategy

How It Works

DAMMstable deploys capital across a diversified set of on-chain yield sources, targeting stable USD-denominated returns with low correlation to broader crypto markets.

The strategy allocates to overcollateralized lending, concentrated liquidity pools, and fixed-yield and yield trading markets, exclusively on blue-chip protocols. Allocation decisions are driven by an internal risk-adjusted framework and supported by algorithmic execution and tooling that monitors yield spreads, protocol liquidity, and on-chain conditions in real time.

All yield is generated natively on-chain, and assets remain on-chain at all times, ensuring full transparency and auditability.
