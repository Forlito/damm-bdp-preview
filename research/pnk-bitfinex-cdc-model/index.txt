<h2>Model — Constant-Depth Curve (CDC)</h2>
<p>Up next is the mathematical formulation for the CD Curve. In essence, it is a function that <strong>maps</strong> the amounts to <strong>bid</strong> and <strong>ask</strong> at different <strong>price</strong> levels given a set of parameters, making it solely dependent on price, and ergo <strong>path-independent</strong> by nature.
