Introducing The Quantitative Edge

DAMM Capital's public research desk. Fund reports, protocol deep dives, and the alpha we choose to share — published in the open, on-chain-verifiable where it counts.

DAMM Capital · announcement

The Quantitative Edge is where DAMM Capital publishes work that is usually kept private: research notes on the protocols we underwrite, monthly fund reports with full methodology, and post-mortems on the strategies we retire.

Everything here follows three rules:

  1. Reproducible — claims come with the data or the query that produced them.
  2. On-chain first — where a statement can be verified against a contract or a transaction, we link it.
  3. No talking our book without saying so — positions and conflicts are disclosed at the top of every post.

New reports land here and are announced on X.
